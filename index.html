Egypt

Egypt has one of the longest histories of any country. The cradle of civilization, ancient Egypt witnessed some of the earliest developments in writing, agriculture, centralized government, and organized religion for more than three millennia before the common era. Iconic monuments such as the Sphinx of Giza, the Pyramids of Giza and the Valley of the Kings reflect this heritage and remain important sources of scholarly and popular interest.
